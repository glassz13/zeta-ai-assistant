SPACE Z - COMPANY OVERVIEW
==========================

Company Name: Space Z Inc.
Founded: 2018
Headquarters: Austin, Texas, USA
CEO: Marcus Hale
CFO: Priya Nandakumar
CTO: Dmitri Volkov
Total Employees: 3,400
Industry: Aerospace & Commercial Space Technology

MISSION STATEMENT
-----------------
Space Z exists to make interplanetary travel accessible, affordable, and sustainable for all of humanity. We believe the future of civilization depends on becoming a multi-planetary species, and we are building the infrastructure to make that future possible within a generation.

CORE DIVISIONS
--------------
1. Launch Services – Orbital and suborbital payload delivery for government and commercial clients
2. Satellite Network (StarGrid) – Low Earth Orbit broadband constellation providing global internet coverage
3. Crewed Missions – Human spaceflight programs including orbital tourism and ISS resupply contracts
4. Propulsion R&D – Next-generation engine development (Helios Engine Series)
5. Space Habitats – Modular orbital station design and manufacturing

KEY ACHIEVEMENTS
----------------
- 2019: First successful launch of Falcon-Z Mk1 rocket
- 2020: Achieved first booster landing and reuse
- 2021: Awarded NASA Commercial Crew Transportation contract worth $1.8B
- 2022: StarGrid Beta launched with 420 satellites in LEO
- 2023: First crewed orbital tourism mission (4 passengers, 9-day mission)
- 2024: Helios Engine prototype achieved 320 seconds of specific impulse in testing
- 2025: StarGrid reached 5,200 active satellites; 1.2 million subscribers globally

CURRENT PROJECTS
----------------
- Ares Heavy: Super-heavy lift vehicle targeting 150-ton LEO payload capacity (launch planned Q3 2026)
- MoonBase Alpha (Phase 1): Lunar surface logistics contract with ESA
- StarGrid Gen2: Next-gen satellites with laser inter-link communication
- Project Hermes: Reusable upper stage for deep space missions
