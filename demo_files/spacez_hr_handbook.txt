SPACE Z - HR POLICIES & EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK SUMMARY
==================================================
Last Updated: January 2026
Document Owner: People Operations Team

EMPLOYMENT TYPES
----------------
Full-Time: Standard 40-hour work week. Eligible for all benefits.
Part-Time: Less than 30 hours/week. Eligible for limited benefits.
Contract: Project-based engagement. Not eligible for standard benefits.
Intern: 12 or 24-week rotational programs. Stipend + housing allowance provided.

COMPENSATION & BENEFITS
------------------------
- Base salary reviewed annually every April
- Performance bonuses paid in October (up to 20% of annual salary)
- Equity grants (RSUs) issued to all full-time employees at hire; 4-year vesting schedule with 1-year cliff
- Health Insurance: Medical, Dental, Vision covered at 90% for employee, 70% for dependents
- 401(k) with 5% company match (vesting immediate)
- Life insurance: 2x annual salary
- Mental health benefit: $1,500/year for therapy or wellness services
- Gym/fitness reimbursement: $600/year

PAID TIME OFF
-------------
- Vacation: 15 days/year (Years 1-3), 20 days/year (Years 4+)
- Sick Leave: 10 days/year, non-rollover
- Personal Days: 3 days/year
- Holidays: 12 federal holidays observed
- Parental Leave: 16 weeks fully paid (primary caregiver), 6 weeks (secondary caregiver)
- Bereavement: 5 days for immediate family, 3 days for extended family

REMOTE & HYBRID WORK POLICY
-----------------------------
- Engineering, Operations, and Mission Control roles: On-site required 5 days/week
- Product, Design, Finance, HR: Hybrid (3 days on-site minimum)
- Sales, Marketing, Partnerships: Flexible remote with monthly team anchor weeks
- All employees must be available during core hours: 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM local HQ time

PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT
-----------------------
- Reviews: Mid-year (June) and Annual (December)
- Performance rated on 5-point scale: Exceptional / Strong / Meets Expectations / Developing / Unsatisfactory
- Two consecutive "Developing" ratings trigger a Performance Improvement Plan (PIP)
- Promotions require manager nomination + HR panel review

CODE OF CONDUCT
---------------
Space Z maintains a zero-tolerance policy for:
  - Discrimination based on race, gender, age, religion, sexual orientation, or disability
  - Workplace harassment (verbal, physical, or digital)
  - Conflict of interest without prior written disclosure
  - Unauthorized sharing of confidential or proprietary information

Violations should be reported to hr@spacez.com or via the anonymous ethics hotline: 1-800-SZ-ETHIC

LEARNING & DEVELOPMENT
-----------------------
- Annual L&D budget per employee: $2,000
- Access to internal Space Z Academy (online courses, workshops, certifications)
- Leadership development cohort (applications open every Q1)
- Conference attendance: Approved by manager; costs covered by department budget

ONBOARDING
-----------
- All new hires complete a 4-week onboarding program
- Includes: safety certifications, security clearance (if applicable), systems access setup, buddy program assignment
- New hires receive a Space Z welcome kit and $500 home office stipend

IT & SECURITY
-------------
- All devices must use Space Z-approved VPN when accessing internal systems remotely
- Passwords must be 14+ characters and rotated every 90 days
- MFA required on all accounts
- Personal devices may not connect to internal Space Z networks without IT approval
